# Products & Platforms

A selection of products I’ve worked on across games, fintech, and marketplaces — spanning early-stage startups through to global-scale platforms with millions of users.

---

## 🎮 FarmVille (Zynga)

**Overview**  
One of the most successful social games of all time, played on Facebook and mobile, centred around farming simulation and social gameplay loops.

**Scale & Metrics**  
- Peaked at ~32M Daily Active Users (DAU)  
- Hundreds of millions of registered users globally  
- Generated billions in lifetime revenue  

**Product Focus**  
- Live operations and monetisation systems  
- Experimentation at scale (events, offers, retention loops)  
- Balancing game economy and player progression  

**Platform**  
- Social Web (Facebook)
