# Company Backgrounds

---

## Tilt (Retro Labs Ltd)

- **What they do:** Livestream shopping marketplace — sellers host live video streams, buyers purchase in real time
- **Stage:** Early-stage startup
- **Funding:** £18m Series A
- **Scale:** ~£4m GMV/month at time of joining
- **Notable:** One of the founding product managers

---

## Stenn Technologies

- **What they do:** Digital trade finance platform providing invoice financing and working capital to SMEs engaged in international trade
- **Stage:** Late-stage scaleup (went into administration Dec 2024)
- **Funding:** ~$950m total raised; $900m valuation at last equity round (2022)
- **Scale:** 200+ employees globally; deployed $10bn+ in capital to customers in 70+ countries; ranked in FT1000 Europe's Fastest Growing Companies three years running
- **Notable:** Backed by HSBC, Barclays, Goldman Sachs, Natixis, Centerbridge

---

## Shell

- **What they do:** Global energy company — one of the world's largest
- **Context:** Role was embedded within Shell's internal innovation hub, working across downstream, trading and supply, IT, and design functions
- **Stage:** Large enterprise / FTSE listed

---

## King (King Digital Entertainment)

- **What they do:** Mobile and social games developer, best known for Candy Crush Saga and Farm Heroes Saga
- **Stage:** Public company (NYSE: KING); acquired by Activision Blizzard for ~$6bn in 2016
- **Scale:** 200m+ MAU across portfolio at peak; $2bn+ in annual revenue
- **Product worked on:** Farm Heroes Saga — match-3 mobile puzzle game; first UK-developed mobile game to surpass $1bn in lifetime revenue; 50m+ players; now over $2bn lifetime revenue
- **Notable:** King was headquartered in London; Farm Heroes Saga was its second-biggest franchise after Candy Crush

---

## Zynga

- **What they do:** Social and mobile games developer, best known for FarmVille, CityVille, and Words With Friends
- **Stage:** Public company (NASDAQ: ZNGA); acquired by Take-Two Interactive for ~$12.7bn in 2022
- **Scale:** 300m+ monthly active users at peak; one of the largest social games companies in the world
- **Products worked on:**
  - **FarmVille** — Zynga's flagship product; peaked at 32M DAU; one of the most-played social games in history
  - **CastleVille** — 11M DAU, $400k/day revenue
  - **CoasterVille** — grew to ~6M DAU, $300k/day revenue under Heather's direction
- **Notable:** Role spanned Game Designer through to Senior Director / General Manager across multiple titles

---

## Turbine (now Standing Stone Games)

- **What they do:** MMORPG developer and operator, focused on persistent online worlds based on major IP
- **Stage:** Originally part of Warner Bros. Interactive; later transitioned to independent studio (Standing Stone Games)
- **Scale:** Millions of registered players across titles; long-running live service games
- **Products worked on:**
  - **The Lord of the Rings Online** — long-running MMORPG set in Tolkien’s Middle-earth; hybrid free-to-play/subscription model
  - **Dungeons & Dragons Online** — one of the first major MMOs to successfully transition from subscription to free-to-play
- **Notable:** Both titles are enduring live-service products with over a decade of continuous operation, requiring ongoing content delivery, monetisation tuning, and community management
